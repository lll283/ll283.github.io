Chain of Command

  • Founders — Absolute authority
  • Board — Oversight & governance
  • Executives — Server-wide operations
  • Management — Staff control
  • Internal Affairs — Investigations
  • Administration — Punishments & logs
  • Moderation — Player moderation
  • Trainees — Observe only

Division Responsibilities

  • Management: Promotions, demotions, inactivity
  • Internal Affairs: Corruption, abuse, appeals
  • Administration: Kicks, bans, Melonly entries
  • Moderation: Chat, minor rule enforcement

ER:LC Core Rules

  • No RDM / VDM
  • No Fail RP
  • No Metagaming
  • No exploiting / glitch abuse

Punishments

  • Minor → Warning / Jail
  • Moderate → Kick / Temp Ban
  • Severe → Permanent Ban
  • Staff Abuse → Immediate removal

Melonly Logging

  • Every punishment logged
  • Evidence mandatory
  • No log = invalid action

OST

OST members follow Foundership directives and operate under IA review.

Final Authority

Ownership may change this handbook at any time.
